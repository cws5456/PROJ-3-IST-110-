Glossary
The Financial Terms of money experts worldwide!

Money Terms

Cash flow
The way money goes in and out of the business.
Aperture
Investors are good assets to businesses because there are people willing to put their money into a business for it to grow and flourish.
Investments
When the investors of the business decide to use the money and put them into other businesses and gain profit.

More Terms

Bankruptcy
Having financial issues such as being stuck in debt can cause the bank which will make the business potentially go out.
Profit
In the financial spectrum it's a benefit to help with more financial opportunities.
Expenses
The amount of times and money spent.
Credit
The trust that an individual will pay back the dues from obtaining products, good, etc.
Credit Score
The number of how well an indiviual is trusted to pay back the dues from having credit.
Credit Limit
The maximum amount of credit an establishment offer to an individual with credit.
Balance Sheet
The display of the overall liabilities, assets showing the balance of income and expenditure over a period of time.
Interest Rate
The portion of a loan that is potetnially held as the interest to the borrower, expressed as a percentage of the loan.
Net Worth
The assets minus the liabilities which all sums out to the worth, or value of a company or individual.
Tax
A contribution to state revenue benefiting workers' income, and additions to the prices of goods and services.
401K
A plan for employees to save and invest in themselves for future retirement.
403B
Mainly for public education employees this is a plan for retirement.
Collateral
Security plan for the repayment of a loan.
